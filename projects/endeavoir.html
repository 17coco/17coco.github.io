Endeavoir Project - A Companion of Your Musical Endeavor

Features

  • Time Tracker: Log and monitor overall time spent on specific subjects, with the option to live track a session or record details manually afterward.
  • Session Log: Review detailed logs of all practice sessions, categorized by subject, to track progress and areas for improvement.
  • Integrated Metronome: Optional feature during live tracking, enabling users to stay on beat with adjustable tempo and sound settings.
  • Goal Setting and Management: Set personalized practice goals and track progress with visual indicators.
  • Practice Randomizer: A randomizer to prevent neglecting any specific area by suggesting exercises or tasks.
  • Repertoire Record: Store and organize learned pieces, linking them with specific practice sessions for focused tracking.
  • Achievements and Milestones: Celebrate progress with visual representations of hours logged, goals achieved, and milestones reached.

Purpose and Vision

Endeavoir is a digital companion for musicians, dedicated to recording, tracking, and celebrating their musical journey. By combining the essence of "endeavor" and "repertoire/reservoir," Endeavoir is designed to be a central hub for documenting every effort, achievement, and milestone on the path to musical mastery.

We believe that every aspiring musician deserves a structured yet creative way to grow. Inspired by the saying, "10,000 hours of practice makes you a master of anything," Endeavoir empowers musicians to track their progress, set meaningful goals, and explore their potential. Through thoughtful design and music-specific tools, Endeavoir not only supports disciplined practice but also fosters creativity and a deep connection to one's craft.
